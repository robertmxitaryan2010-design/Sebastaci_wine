About Our School

Sebastatsi Wine is produced by students of the Mkhitar Sebastatsi Educational Complex.

Our school has a long history of academic excellence and practical education. Through the winemaking project, students gain real-world experience in viticulture, winemaking, marketing, and teamwork.

This project is not only about making wine — it's about building skills, confidence, and a love for our culture and heritage.

About Us

MSKH Wine is a student educational project of the Mkhitar Sebastatsi Educational Complex, created to preserve winemaking traditions and promote Armenian wine culture.

Our mission is to combine knowledge, practice, and respect for nature to produce high-quality wines.
